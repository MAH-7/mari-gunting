  ┌─────────────────┐
  │ INCOME SOURCES  │
  └─────────────────┘

  3️⃣  Travel Fees           → 0% (100% goes to barber)

┌──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                         🚗 TRAVEL COST BREAKDOWN                             │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

  Distance         Formula                      Cost
  ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  0 - 4 km    →    Base Fee                  =  RM 5.00
  4+ km       →    RM 5 + (distance-4)×RM1   =  RM 5 + extra

  Examples:
  ✓ 2 km  = RM 5.00    (base fee)
  ✓ 4 km  = RM 5.00    (base fee)
  ✓ 6 km  = RM 7.00    (5 + 2×1)
  ✓ 10 km = RM 11.00   (5 + 6×1)

  💡 100% of travel fees go to barber (no commission)
